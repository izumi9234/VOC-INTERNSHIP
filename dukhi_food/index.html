Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil for noodles.
  2. In a saucepan, warm stock with soy sauce, mirin, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Lower heat and simmer 5 minutes to let flavors meld.
  4. Cook ramen noodles per package (usually 2–3 minutes). Drain.
  5. Divide noodles into bowls; ladle hot broth over them.
  6. Top with chashu/chicken, halved soft-boiled eggs, and desired toppings.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning with more shoyu or stock as needed.
  8. Serve immediately while steaming hot.
